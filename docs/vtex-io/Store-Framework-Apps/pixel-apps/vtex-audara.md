## Setup

Open a terminal and type:

```sh
vtex install vtex.audara@1.x
```

Open your admin in the Apps section:

`https://YOURSTORENAME.myvtex.com/admin/apps/vtex.audara@1.0.0/setup`

Fill in your Audara's Account ID and click save.
